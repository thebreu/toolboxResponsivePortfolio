Portfolio

Fridays On The Plaza

I was looking at the promotional work that our local library put up for the Friday On The Plaza event in Cheyenne and I felt that i could improve it.
My friend Bobby is playing at that event and is highly photogenic.
I took an image from Bobbys' facebook page, increased the contrast of the image and carefully created vector shapes from the shadows provided by the up in contrast.
I was quite pleased with the result!

Seamore Eye Guy

This little guy was one of my challenges that I did for The Array School of Technology And Design.
We had to create a realistic image of something using only vector shapes in Sketch.
I was trying to find a use for the pencil tool in Sketch as there seemed to be very little use for it in designing. However, I found using the pencil tool and applying a fill to the path I created
made for an excellent texture that I was looking for in the Iris!

Login Mockup

The students at array were tasked with designing a login page for an application. I decided to go for a short and simple page that creates a sense of adventure within itself.
the jagged edges of the banner not only adds to the sense of adventure, but are also reminiscent of mountains.
The challenge of designing something with the mindset of having to create it afterwords really made me think about how to make it simple yet visually striking.

Wasabi Grill & Bar

I was approached by Wasabi Grill & Bar to do a redesign of their website.
Their previous website was created poorly and the images used were rushed. (top image)
I took their current logo and made them a custom vector image of their logo. I didnt want this issue to arise again. I also took into account that Wasabi was not some fast food resturant and their current logo did not represent the elegance and feel of thier actual resturant.
I recomended the use of the new color schemes for thier logo to better represent that.
